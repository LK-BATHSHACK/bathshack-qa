Work through this yourself before submitting for QA review. These are the things you should be catching first.
Copy & brand voice
  • Read the copy aloud — it sounds like a real person, not a press release
  • No generic AI phrases: "elevate", "seamless", "perfect solution", "take your bathroom to the next level"
  • No short choppy sentences laid out like a bullet-point list
  • Tone is warm, helpful and expert — fun where it fits, never pushy
  • Subject line is specific to Bathshack — it could not have come from any other retailer
  • Preview text adds something new — it does not repeat the subject line
UK & IE compliance
  • UK and IE are completely separate sends — no crossover whatsoever
  • Currency correct: £ for UK, € for IE
  • UK free delivery threshold is exactly £499 — not £299, £350 or any other figure
  • IE free delivery threshold is exactly €699 — not any other figure
  • Both markets use www.bathshack.com — URL verified
  • New Site Launch promotion (3–30 June) referenced correctly if applicable
  • Bathshack-provided sale designs used — not custom agency designs
  • No UK offers in IE emails and no IE offers in UK emails
Delivery & service claims
  • No next-day delivery promises anywhere in copy or design
  • No "next-day dispatch", "delivered tomorrow" or "instant delivery" language
  • No "guaranteed next-day shipping" language
  • Approved delivery language used where needed
  • NI delivery (if referenced) noted correctly — faster than UK mainland
Product accuracy
  • All featured products checked as live on bathshack.com right now
  • Products reference active categories only
  • No discontinued products referenced — e.g. the bathroom planner
  • Any stated pricing verified against the live site
Design & assets
  • Desktop Figma screenshot exported and ready to upload
  • Mobile Figma screenshot exported and ready to upload
  • CTA is prominent, clearly labelled and easy to tap on mobile
  • Logo usage follows brand guidelines
  • Imagery is lifestyle-led and on-brand
  • No pricing or compliance claims visible in the design that differ from copy
